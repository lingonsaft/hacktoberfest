Artic Fox

The Arctic fox has a keen sense of smell. They can smell carcasses that are often left by polar bears anywhere from 10-40km. It is possible that they use their sense of smell to also track down polar bears.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes lagopus
  • Class : Mammal
  • Order : Carnivore
  • Habitat : Artic Tundra

The Arctic fox is a small fox native to the Arctic regions of the Northern Hemisphere and common throughout the Arctic tundra biome. It is well adapted to living in cold environments, and is best known for its thick, warm fur that is also used as camouflage. On average, Arctic foxes only live 3-4 years in the wild.
