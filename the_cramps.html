The Cramps

Bad Music for Bad People

    History

  • American rockabilly band, formed in 1976 and active until 2009
  • Split after the death of lead singer Lux Interior
  • Part of the early CBGB punk rock movement that had emerged in New York
  • Recognized as one of the prime innovators of psychobilly
  • Focus on camp humor, sexual double-entendre, and retro horror/sci-fi b-movie iconography
  • "Psychobilly" was a term coined by The Cramps
